England has won their second World CUP!

After 84 years of waiting, dreaming, singing, believing, doubting, and saying “this is the year. It's coming home!” England have finally done it and won the FIFA World Cup again.

Englands Road to Glory

England's tournament was far from easy. They topped their group after wins against Japan, Ghana and draw against Canada. In the knockout rounds, they beat Portugal, France, Argentina, and finally Brazil. The semifinal against Argentina was especially tense, ending in a penalty shootout that England won 7-6.

Key factors that lead to victory

  • Strong defensive performances
  • Moments of magic from Anthony Bellingham and Keeper Michael Watford
  • Impeccable Teamwork
  • Turning up in crucial moments

Golden Generation

This England team had everything: pace, creativity, leadership, defensive strength, and most importantly, belief. For decades, England had been accused of having great players but not great teams. In 2050, that changed.
